What to do in České Budějovice

Budweiser Budvar Brewery

Founded in 1895, it produces the original Czech beer [cite: 1089-1090]. The tour includes a direct tasting for 290 CZK (Students: 220 CZK) [cite: 1097-1102].

View tour

Hluboká Castle

Inspired by Windsor, this neo-Gothic castle is one of the most beautiful in the country [cite: 1103-1105]. Adult entry: 300 CZK; Teens: 240 CZK [cite: 1116-1121].

View castle

Motor České Budějovice

Experience the intensity of ice hockey at the Budvar Arena [cite: 1122-1123]. Standing tickets from 260 CZK, only 100 CZK with an ISIC card [cite: 1132-1133].

Enjoy nature and city views at sunset. Ideal for beginners on 1 to 2-hour trips [cite: 1134-1135].
